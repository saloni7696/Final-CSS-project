About Us

Why the name Moum?

Well, it's not just a name; it's a nod to our roots and a touch of personal flair. 'Moum,' born from the Hindi word for wax, is a celebration of history and the fragrant imprints it left behind.

Who are we?

Moum is more than just a candle brand; it's the realization of a lifelong dream. Our journey began with a vision to craft products that ingeniously solve real problems. Melding creativity with a deep desire for sustainable and purposeful decor, Moum emerged.At Moum, we've redefined the art of candle-making, introducing a touch of brilliance to the ordinary. Our pearl wax candles stand as a testament to ingenuity, offering a clever way to repurpose existing containers into endlessly customizable, safer, and more sustainable candles.
